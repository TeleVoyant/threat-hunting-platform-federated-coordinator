# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org),
version 2.1.

In short: we pledge to make participation a harassment-free experience for everyone,
regardless of background or identity. Be respectful, assume good faith, and focus on
what is best for the community and the security of the federation.

Examples of unacceptable behaviour include harassment, personal or political attacks,
publishing others' private information, and other conduct reasonably considered
inappropriate in a professional setting.

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and may remove, edit, or reject
contributions, comments, and contributors that violate this Code of Conduct.

To report unacceptable behaviour, contact the maintainers listed in the repository
metadata. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

The full text of the Contributor Covenant 2.1 is available at
https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/
